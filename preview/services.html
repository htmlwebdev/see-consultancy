Consultancy

Consultancy Services

We provide specialised energy economics, commercial advisory, and strategic consulting that supports clients across the full energy value chain — combining rigorous analytical methods with practical, real-world insight.

Investment Analysis & Valuation

  • Asset and project valuation using DCF, comparables, and scenario-based methods
  • Assessment of investment viability, value drivers, and downside risks
  • Evaluation of capital structure, returns, and cost of capital assumptions
  • Support for investment approvals and decision-making frameworks

Project Economics & Financial Modeling

  • Transparent, auditable project-level financial models
  • CAPEX, OPEX, production, and revenue forecasting across project life
  • Integration of financing structures, taxes, and cash-flow waterfalls
  • Sensitivity and scenario testing to assess project robustness

Risk Analysis & Sensitivity Assessment

  • Identification of key market, operational, financial, and regulatory risks
  • Sensitivity analysis on prices, costs, volumes, and timing assumptions
  • Scenario and stress testing under adverse market conditions
  • Quantification of risk impacts on project and portfolio returns

Transaction Support & Due Diligence

  • Commercial and financial due diligence for M&A and investments
  • Independent review of assumptions, models, and market positioning
  • Identification of value drivers, risks, and potential deal breakers
  • Support for transaction negotiations and investment committees

Deal Structuring & Commercial Advisory

  • Structuring of commercial terms to optimise risk–return outcomes
  • Evaluation of contractual, fiscal, and revenue-sharing mechanisms
  • Support for joint ventures, partnerships, and financing structures
  • Alignment of deal economics with strategic and financial objectives

Portfolio Services & Optimization

  • Portfolio-level performance analysis across assets and markets
  • Risk-adjusted return assessment and capital-allocation support
  • Diversification, optimisation, and rebalancing opportunities
  • Portfolio scenario analysis under changing market and policy conditions

Asset Optimization & Performance Analysis

  • Operational and financial performance benchmarking of energy assets
  • Identification of cost reduction and efficiency improvement opportunities
  • Optimisation of production, dispatch, and revenue strategies
  • Continuous performance monitoring and value enhancement

Trading Advisory & Market Execution

  • Advisory on physical and financial trading strategies
  • Support for hedging structures, spread trading, and portfolio positioning
  • Evaluation of price, basis, and liquidity risk
  • Alignment of trading strategies with commercial and risk objectives

Data Analytics & Decision Support

  • Data-driven analytical models for energy markets and assets
  • Integration of operational, market, and financial data
  • Scenario analysis and dashboards to support management decisions
  • Automation and optimisation of reporting and analytics workflows

Market Research, Analysis & Forecasting

  • In-depth market research across oil, gas, LNG, power, and renewables
  • Demand–supply analysis and short- to long-term price forecasting
  • Identification of market trends, structural shifts, and policy impacts
  • Forecast-driven support for investment timing and strategic planning

Strategy Development & Growth Planning

  • Corporate and business-unit strategy formulation
  • Long-term growth planning and capital-allocation frameworks
  • Evaluation of organic and inorganic growth opportunities
  • Strategic roadmap development aligned with market evolution

Market Entry & Expansion Strategy

  • Market entry assessment including regulatory and commercial analysis
  • Evaluation of entry modes, partnerships, and investment structures
  • Risk assessment for new geographies and market segments
  • Execution-focused recommendations for sustainable expansion

Training Services

Collaborating through our partners, we deliver practical, industry-focused training programs designed to build deep technical and commercial capability across the global energy sector. Onsite, online, or hybrid.

Applied Energy Economics & Financial Modeling

  • Practical, application-driven training focused on real decision-making
  • Hands-on Excel and financial modelling across energy projects and assets
  • Project economics, valuation, cash-flow modelling, and sensitivity analysis
  • Best-practice model structuring with clear assumptions and logic

Real Industry Case Studies

  • Real-world oil, gas, LNG, power, and renewable energy cases
  • Translation of theory into commercial and investment decisions
  • Discussion of market challenges, outcomes, and lessons learned
  • Group-based case exercises to reinforce applied understanding

Energy Markets, Trading & Hedging Workshops

  • Market fundamentals across oil, gas, LNG, power, and energy derivatives
  • Trading-strategy simulations: spreads, arbitrage, and positioning
  • Hedging workshops using futures, swaps, and options
  • Practical understanding of price, basis, volume, and liquidity risk

Portfolio Optimisation & Investment Strategy

  • Portfolio-level risk–return analysis across energy assets
  • Optimisation exercises covering diversification and capital allocation
  • Scenario and stress testing under volatile market conditions
  • Linking portfolio insights to investment and strategic decisions

Tailored Corporate & Executive Training

  • Customised programs aligned to client roles and business objectives
  • Domain-specific training for trading, investment, development, and utility teams
  • Executive sessions on strategy, risk, and capital allocation
  • Modular delivery — workshops, bootcamps, or extended programs

Practical Learning & Capability Building

  • Strong emphasis on hands-on learning and real market data
  • Model-driven sessions enabling participants to build and modify tools
  • Focus on immediately applicable skills and frameworks
  • Knowledge transfer to support long-term internal capability
