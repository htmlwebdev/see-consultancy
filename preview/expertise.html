Capability map

Where we go deep

Nine focus areas spanning conventional hydrocarbons, renewables, financial markets and capability-building. Each is grounded in real engagements and live market data.

1

Oil & Gas Fiscal Modelling & Energy Economics (PSC & JV Frameworks)

  • Upstream fiscal modelling: PSCs, royalties, cost recovery, R-Factor, ring-fencing
  • Integrated production, cost, and revenue forecasting across field life
  • Economic limit testing (ELT), abandonment, and decommissioning analysis
  • Screening, ranking, and marginal field evaluation under multiple scenarios
2

Renewable Energy & Power Markets

  • Economic modelling for solar, wind, hybrid, and battery-storage projects
  • Power market fundamentals, pricing, and dispatch logic
  • LCOE, LCOS, and revenue-stack evaluation
  • Regulatory, policy, and market-design assessment across regions
3

Financial Modelling & Investment Analysis

  • Full DCF, APV, and scenario-based valuation
  • WACC estimation, leverage analysis, and tax-shield evaluation
  • Portfolio-level value assessment and capital-allocation analysis
  • Investment screening and comparative opportunity assessment
4

Project Finance & Debt Structuring

  • Project-finance modelling for LNG, power, renewables, and storage
  • Debt sizing, DSCR/LLCR analysis, and financing optimisation
  • Capital-structure design and funding-strategy support
  • Lender-focused sensitivity, downside, and covenant analysis
5

Market Research & Price Forecasting

  • Oil, gas, LNG, and power market outlooks
  • Benchmark pricing analysis (Brent, WTI, Dubai, JKM, HH)
  • Freight and tanker market analysis (Worldscale, chartering, demurrage)
  • Power markets & energy-transition dynamics
6

M&A Strategy & Commercial Advisory

  • Commercial and financial due diligence
  • Asset screening, benchmarking, and competitive analysis
  • Transaction, divestment, and partnership support
  • Contracting strategy for long-term sales, tolling, and marketing
7

Data Analytics, Forecasting & Digital Optimisation

  • Time-series, econometric, and scenario-based forecasting
  • Statistical and machine-learning applications in energy markets
  • Python, Excel, R, and Power BI-based analytical solutions
  • Automation of financial, economic, and reporting workflows
8

Trading, Risk Management & Derivatives

  • Energy derivatives: futures, options, swaps, swaptions
  • Risk management & hedging programs across price, basis, and volume risk
  • Spread trading: crack, spark, dark, and time spreads
  • VaR, ETL, Monte Carlo simulation, and stress testing
9

Executive Training & Capability Building

  • Petroleum economics and project-evaluation training
  • Oil, gas, LNG, and power market fundamentals
  • Energy finance, valuation, and risk management
  • Custom in-house programs for analysts, managers, and executives
